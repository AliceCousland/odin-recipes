Chocolate-Banana Soy Smoothie

Description

Try soy milk with Carnation Breakfast Essentials® Light Start™ to start off your morning right. Take a look at this Chocolate-Banana Soy Smoothie today!

Serves 1 person

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup chocolate soy milk
  • 1 packet Rich Milk Chocolate Flavor CARNATION BREAKFAST ESSENTIALS® LIGHT START™ Complete Nutritional Drink
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup ice cubes

    • Steps

    1. Place soy milk, Carnation Breakfast Essentials Drink, banana and ice in blender; cover. Blend until smooth.
